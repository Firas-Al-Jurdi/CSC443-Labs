Lebanese American Univeristy

Mission

The Lebanese American University is committed to academic excellence, student centeredness, civic engagement,
the advancement of scholarship, the education of the whole person, and the formation of leaders in a diverse world.

Value

  • The Lebanese American University endeavors to act in a manner that is guided by a set of core values
    and a deep-rooted sense of shared ethical values and aspirations that define its character and culture.
    Building on this foundation, LAU:
    • raws its fundamental inspiration from the devotion of its Presbyterian Founders to always seek the truth, respect human dignity,
      promote gender equality, and be inclusive,
    • provides educational opportunities as one university with multiple campuses, each with distinctive strengths and attributes.
    • commits to academic and service excellence,
    • demonstrates dignity and respect for and from the Board of Trustees, faculty, staff, and students in word and in deed.
